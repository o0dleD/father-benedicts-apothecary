Mossy Stone Niche

Life emerges even from the shade of quiet corners.

Aroma: Soft moss, damp earth, faint mineral warmth.

Notes: Oakmoss, Vetiver, Elemi, and Petitgrain.

Sunlit Arch

Shafts of warm light move slowly across the cool flagstones.

Aroma: Warm stone, gentle sun-dusted air, subtle hints of distant blooms.

Notes: Bergamot, Elemi, and Jasmine.

Garden Glimpse

Even from a distance, the garden tempts us to enter.

Aroma: Fresh green edge, faint floral lift, softened by stone.

Notes: Green leaves, Rosemary, Tangerine, and Petitgrain.

Air of Incense

The air holds the subtle scent of distant worship.

Aroma: Whisper of incense, soft warm stone, early-spring pollen hints.

Notes: Frankincense, Cade, and Vetiver.

The Cloister

Soft afternoon sunlight filters through the arches, warming both stone and hidden stems. Walk the cloistered paths, pass between sun and shadow, and let the fragrant spring air calm your mind.

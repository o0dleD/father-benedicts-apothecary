    Create a Unique Blend

    This page allows you to create your own signature scent using a curated selection of Father Benedict’s holy accords.

    You may select up to eight accords, selecting at least one accord from each section: Top, Heart, Base. A total of five or six accords is often the most harmonious choice.

    Each commission is bespoke: the selected accords form only the beginning of a longer process of balance, refinement, and quiet contemplation.

    A fixed donation to the monastery applies, reflecting the time required to bring the blend into true accord.

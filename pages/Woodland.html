Woodland

Among towering oaks and mossed earth, sunlight filters through a canopy of tender leaves. Blossoms drift on the breeze, and hidden paths hint at the presence of creatures unseen. Wander slowly, breathe deeply, and let the Woodland awaken a quiet wonder in the rhythms of spring.

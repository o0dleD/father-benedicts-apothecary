Water's Edge

Still waters reveal what the heart has long known; even the quietest currents speak truth.

Aroma: Fresh, mineral, cool, subtly green.

Notes: Vetiver, Elemi, and Galbanum.

Sunlit Meadow

The sun gilds even the humblest grass; attention turns the ordinary into a revelation.

Aroma: Warm, sun-baked greenery, lightly floral.

Notes: Lavender, Bergamot, Geranium, and Chamomile.

Willow Trees

Beneath the bending branches, one finds the patience to listen.

Aroma: Freshly leafy, slightly balsamic, with a hint of earthiness.

Notes: Petitgrain, Cypress, and Cedarwood.

Fallen Branches

Even that which falls nourishes the soil; decay is but a whisper of life renewed.

Aroma: Woody, resinous, dry, lightly smoky.

Notes: Guaiacwood, Birch Tar, Cade, and Frankincense.

The Riverbank

Along the gently flowing river, golden light warms the grasses and wildflowers at the water’s edge. Willow branches bend gracefully, and driftwood tells the story of seasons past. Pause here, listen to the water, and let the Riverbank guide your thoughts toward stillness and reflection.

Frosted Stone Walk

Cold clarifies what warmth allows us to ignore.

Aroma: Cold mineral air, icy stone, faint moss beneath frost.

Notes: Vetiver, oakmoss, Pine, and Angelica Root.

Snow-covered Wall

Life waits, perfectly intact, beneath the white.

Aroma: Frozen green stems, snow-damp air, distant herbs silenced by cold.

Notes: Galbanum, Fir Needles, Sage, and Vetiver.

Incense in Cold Air

Even smoke moves slowly in the cold.

Aroma: Cool frankincense, dry smoke, breath fogging the stillness.

Notes: Frankincense, elemi, Cade, and Angelica Seed.

Chapel Door

Some thresholds open only after waiting.

Aroma: Aged wood, frozen iron, faint warmth held deep within.

Notes: Cedarwood, Myrrh, Oakwood, and Black Pepper.

The Winter Cloister

Snow drifts outside frost-lined arches, light sharp and silvery. Walk the quiet paths, hear the soft hush of winter air, and let the Winter Cloister bring clarity and calm.

Blossom Canopy

Every blossom opens with courage; the eye that sees it, too, is renewed.

Aroma: Lightly sweet, floral, airy.

Notes: Apple Blossom, Pear Blossom, and Linden Blossom.

Fruitful Branches

The fruit is earned by the patient climb; ripeness is measured in calm observation.

Aroma: Juicy, fresh, lightly tart, sun-warmed.

Notes: Bergamot, Orange, Lemon, and Green Apple.

Grass & Wildflowers

Even at the orchard’s feet, miracles flourish quietly; notice them, and they bloom twice.

Aroma: Herbaceous, lightly sweet, green.

Notes: Clary Sage, Chamomile, Elemi, and Sweet Grass.

Worn Stone Path

Where many have walked, the earth remembers; worn stones hold the whispers of time.

Aroma: Dry, mineral, slightly mossy, grounding.

Notes: Oakmoss, Vetiver, Patchouli, and Elemi.

The Orchard

Rows of fruit trees stretch beneath a sunlit sky, blossoms mingling with ripening apples and pears. Grass and wildflowers sway in a gentle breeze, and a worn stone path winds among the trees. Stroll here, notice each bloom and fruit, and let the Orchard awaken your senses to the sweetness of careful attention.
